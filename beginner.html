Getting Started with Your TI-84+CE

Beginner Basics

To power on your TI-84+CE, press the ON key at the bottom left. Use the arrow keys to move around menus and options. Press 2ND + ON to turn it off.

The 2ND key gives you access to the secondary (yellow) function of each button. It’s how you open menus like “QUIT” (2ND + MODE), “ANGLE” (2ND + APPS), and more. You’ll use it constantly to navigate and access hidden calculator tools.

The ALPHA key lets you type letters and access the green-labeled functions printed above certain keys. For instance, pressing ALPHA + MATH can open shortcuts for variable names or commands. Holding it down lets you type multiple letters at once.

Use the arrow keys to scroll through lists, navigate menus, and adjust graphing windows. When editing equations or programs, LEFT and RIGHT move your cursor precisely, while UP and DOWN browse history or switch lines.

To input text, press ALPHA followed by any key that has a green letter printed above it. This is essential when naming variables, labeling graphs, or entering notes. To lock Alpha mode, press 2ND + ALPHA.

Press the Y= button to open the graphing menu. Enter functions like Y1 = X² and hit GRAPH to see it appear. You can use WINDOW to control the viewing area and ZOOM to adjust automatically.
